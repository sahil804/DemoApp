Glossary:

This app is about showing different MENU ITEMS in a LOOP. EACH ITEM WILL SHOW IN A SCREEN FOR A PARTICULAR INTERVAL.
1. Triangle: PIZZA
2. RECTANGLE: HOTDOG
3. CIRCLE: CHAPATI
4. RED: RED CHILLI TOPPING
5. GREEN: GREEN CHILLI TOPPING
6. YELLOW: YELLOW CAPSICUM TOPPING
7. 5 SECONDS: ITEM_INTERVAL
8. TRIANGLE to RECTANGLE: MENU ITEM SWITCH
9. MENUITEM: PIZZA, HOTDOG, CHAPATI
10.INGREDIENTS: RED CHILLI TOPPING, GREEN CHILLI TOPPING, YELLOW CAPSICUM TOPPING
